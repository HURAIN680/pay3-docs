Merchant payments let your AI act as a fully autonomous business payment agent — paying supplier invoices, settling vendor bills, covering SaaS subscriptions, and purchasing API access, all in USDC on Stellar, without you having to log into a payment portal or approve every line item manually. Your AI identifies the recipient, resolves their Stellar address from your saved contacts, validates the payment against your spending policy, and executes the transfer — giving you a complete audit trail in your Pay3 dashboard when it's done.

## How it works

When your AI needs to pay a merchant, it calls Pay3's `transfer` MCP tool with the merchant's name or alias. Pay3 handles the rest:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Recipient resolution">
    Pay3 looks up the merchant name in your saved contacts and resolves it to a verified Stellar address. If the name is ambiguous or not found, your AI stops and asks you for clarification — Pay3 never guesses a payment recipient.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Policy validation">
    Pay3 checks that the merchant is on your allowed recipients list, confirms the payment asset (USDC) is permitted, and verifies the amount is within your daily budget and per-transaction limit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execution decision">
    If the payment is within your autonomous limit, Pay3 executes immediately. If it exceeds your approval threshold, it queues the payment for your review in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transaction submitted to Stellar">
    Pay3 signs the transaction with your session key and submits it to the Stellar network. Settlement typically completes in 3–5 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Your AI confirms the payment with the transaction ID, amount, recipient, and timestamp. The record appears in your Pay3 transaction history for audit purposes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example: paying a supplier invoice

Here's what a real merchant payment looks like end-to-end:

```text
User:   "Pay the USDC invoice from Acme Supplies — 250 USDC"

AI      → calls Pay3 transfer() MCP tool
          recipient: "Acme Supplies"
          asset: "USDC"
          amount: "250"

Pay3    → resolves "Acme Supplies" from saved contacts
          → found: GDXT...7KQP (verified Stellar address)
          → policy check: 250 USDC within daily budget ✓
          → per-transaction limit: 250 USDC ✓
          → approval threshold: 250 USDC ✓ (auto-executes)
          → session key signs transaction
          → submitted to Stellar

AI      → "Done — 250 USDC sent to Acme Supplies.
            Transaction ID: abc123. Your daily budget
            remaining: 750 USDC."
```

<Note>
  Merchant payments above your manual approval threshold are automatically queued for your review in the Pay3 dashboard. Your AI will inform you that approval is required and wait — it will not retry or find a workaround. Pending approvals expire after **2 minutes**, so check your dashboard promptly when your AI flags one.
</Note>

## Policy settings for merchant payments

Merchant payments use the same policy engine as every other Pay3 action. You can restrict payments to a specific list of approved merchants, cap the maximum single payment, and set a monthly vendor budget independently of your DeFi or general spending limits.

<Tip>
  Add your regular suppliers and vendors to your **Saved Contacts** in the Pay3 dashboard before enabling merchant payment automation. Your AI can only pay verified, named contacts — this prevents accidental or unauthorized payments to unknown addresses.
</Tip>

| Setting | What it controls |
|---|---|
| Per-transaction limit | Maximum single merchant payment |
| Daily budget | Total daily spend across all payments |
| Approval threshold | Payments above this value queue for your review |
| Allowed recipients | Restrict payments to specific saved contacts only |
| Allowed assets | Typically USDC for merchant payments |

## Security reminders

- Your AI resolves recipients from your verified contacts only — it will never pay an unknown or unverified Stellar address.
- Ambiguous recipient names always stop the payment and prompt you for clarification.
- Your AI session key expires automatically, and you can revoke all AI access instantly from the Pay3 dashboard using **Emergency Revoke All**.
